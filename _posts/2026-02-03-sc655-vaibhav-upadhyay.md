## Course Content
1. Random variables, density and distribution functions for univariate and multivariate random variables;
2. Gaussian random vectors, minimum mean square error estimation, Kalman filter;
3. Elementary concentration inequalities, Gaussian concentration;
4. Discrete Markov chains, Markov times, ergodicity of Markov chains, sampling;
5. Martingales.
