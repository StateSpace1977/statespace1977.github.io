## Course Content
Basics of linear algebra: vector spaces and matrices
• Solution to linear ODEs: uniqueness, state-transition matrix, Peano-Baker Series, variation of
constants formula
• Stability: Lyapunov stability, stability of linearization, BIBO stability
• Controllability: LTV systems, controllability Gramian, controllability matrix, Hautus test,
Lyapunov test, pole placement.
• Stabilizability: Controllable decomposition, stabilizability tests, state feedback control
• Observability: Observability Gramian, duality for LTI systems, observability tests
• Detectability: Observable decomposition, detectability tests, state estimators, output
feedback control, separation principle
• Kalman decomposition, minimal realization
• Optimal control and /or output regulation problem
