## Final Takeaway
This course is essential for any control engineer. The concepts especially passivity and backstepping are used heavily for many control applications. The main highlight about the course is it does not involve too much in depth complex analysis instead  focusing on grasping  of concepts and its application.  This course also prerequisite for Adaptive control.
