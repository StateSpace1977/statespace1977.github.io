## Course Content
Vector spaces (6 weeks): Linear independence, basis, dimension, subspaces,

linear functionals, dual spaces, linear transformations, inner products, projec-
tions, the least squares problem, normed vector spaces, eigen values and eigen

vectors.
• Real analysis (4 weeks): Sequences, series and convergence, Cauchy sequences,
continuity, tests for convergence, power series, differentiation and integration
of a series.

• Multivariable calculus (4 weeks): Taylor’s formula, differentiation of func-
tions of several variables, Gˆateaux and Frechet derivatives, differentiability

of vector-valued functions, the chain rule, Taylor’s formula for functions of
several variables, the inverse function and implicit function theorems.
